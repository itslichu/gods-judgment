To run our program, after compiling using javac *.java and running with java Main, please input the name of the village you want to have judgement over, as well as how many initial villagers you would like to have in the village (cannot be greater than 36). Afterwards, press the buttons as prompted, listening to your duties from God. Once the village is created, you are free to do whatever you would like! You can instill the plague upon the population, add a murderer to the village, make it rain, remove and add villagers at will, and observe how villagers interact with each other and their environment! You can click on a villager to reveal their characteristics, and your managing of the village will be reflected in the village markers of health and happiness. Once you are done playing the game, click the "Quit Game" Button, and a final screen of the events that occurred in your play through will appear.
